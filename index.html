01 / ABOUT

Who Am I

I'm Santanu Biswas, a cyber security enthusiast building a strong foundation in ethical hacking, network defense, and penetration testing. My interest in security started with a simple question — "how would someone actually break into this?" — and turned into a discipline of learning how systems fail so I can help make them fail less.

I spend my time inside labs like TryHackMe and Hack The Box, reverse-engineering how vulnerabilities work, and translating that knowledge into practical defense. I care about doing this the right way: responsibly, ethically, and in service of people and institutions that need it — including a long-term goal of supporting law enforcement cyber crime units.

Career Objective

To grow into a professional penetration tester and security analyst role where I can proactively identify vulnerabilities, strengthen defensive infrastructure, and contribute to national and organizational cyber resilience.

Education

  • SELF Self-directed training in ethical hacking via hands-on labs and CTFs.
